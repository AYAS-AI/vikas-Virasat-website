Purpose

The purpose of 'Vikas Bhi, Virasat Bhi' is to promote an inclusive vision of nation-building, where rapid economic and infrastructural development is harmonized with the conservation of India’s centuries-old heritage—be it monuments, traditions, languages, art, or indigenous knowledge systems. This approach ensures that as India moves forward on the path of modernization, it does not lose sight of its cultural identity, legacy, and the historical values that define its soul.
