Case Study — Solar City Initiative

Transforming mid-sized cities into self-sustaining solar hubs.

Overview: In 2023, the Solar City Initiative began converting public buildings to solar power, slashing carbon emissions by 60% in 2 years.

Approach: Public-private partnerships, rooftop solar adoption, and community solar gardens.

Impact: Lower energy costs, cleaner environment, and replicable models for other cities.
